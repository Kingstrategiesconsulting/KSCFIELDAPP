Working at Heights

READ CAREFULLY: You must understand all hazards and controls before starting work.

Key Hazards

  • Falls from heights (>2 metres)
  • Falling objects striking workers below
  • Scaffold/platform collapse
  • Weather conditions affecting stability

Controls Required

  • Scaffolding with guardrails (min 900mm height)
  • Fall arrest harness and lanyard (inspected)
  • Exclusion zone established below work area
  • Competent person supervision
  • Weather assessment before commencing
  • Toolbox talk completed

PPE Required

  • Hard hat (Class 1)
  • Safety glasses/goggles
  • High-visibility clothing (Class D/N)
  • Steel-capped boots
  • Fall arrest harness (AS/NZS 1891.1)
  • Gloves (task-appropriate)

Emergency Procedures

  • Stop work immediately if unsafe
  • Call 000 for emergency services
  • Notify supervisor/site manager
  • Administer first aid if safe
  • Preserve scene and complete incident report
