Key Hazards

  • Falls from heights (>2m)
  • Falling objects
  • Scaffold collapse
  • Weather conditions

Controls Required

  • Guardrails (min 900mm)
  • Fall arrest harness
  • Exclusion zone below
  • Competent supervision

PPE Required

  • Hard hat (Class 1)
  • Safety glasses
  • High-vis (Class D/N)
  • Steel-capped boots
